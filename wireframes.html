Two roles, two layouts.

The Oracle is an ancient blind woman who lives in a cave beneath the Crystal Caves. She speaks only in verse and her prophecies are always accurate — but deliberately ambiguous. She predicted Elena's rise before her birth and warned Marcus about the Shadow King's return twenty years before it happened. Despite her immense knowledge, she refuses to intervene directly, claiming that "the threads must be woven by mortal hands." She subsists on mushrooms that grow in the cave and seems to not age. Some believe she is an aspect of the World Tree itself. When angry, the cave trembles.

Code impact: The popup changes from kanban column builders (~150 lines with hardcoded depth) to a recursive tree renderer (~80 lines, unlimited depth) plus a simple entry list and detail panel. The sidebar mini-kanban is ~30 lines — one loop over tree.root.children building read-only category rows. Net effect is less code handling more depth with cleaner separation.
